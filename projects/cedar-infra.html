Cedar Beverage Co. - Full Digital Transformation

To support a manufacturer's vision of becoming a household name across Africa, I led its digital transformation by architecting a new technology stack that connects the company to its customers, ensuring continuous production (via 99.9% uptime) and funding strategic growth (with $200k in annual savings).

Production December 2021 - Present Technical Operations Lead

The Challenge

Legacy Infrastructure

A traditional manufacturing company with no modern technology infrastructure, hindering growth and international expansion plans across Africa.

Unreliable Environment

Operating in regions with unstable power grids and inconsistent internet connectivity, requiring resilient and redundant system design.

Remote Management

Need to architect systems that could be fully managed from the US while supporting 130+ employees across multiple locations in Africa.

The Process & My Contribution

Acting as the de facto CTO, I managed the entire lifecycle from strategic planning to hands-on implementation and remote administration.

1

Needs Analysis & Gap Identification

Conducted comprehensive analysis identifying critical gaps in security, operations, and data management that were hindering growth. Created strategic roadmap for complete digital transformation.

2

Vendor & Cost Management

Consolidated vendors and personally handled procurement and repairs. When a CCTV contractor underperformed, took over the project, self-funding superior networked cameras to ensure quality outcome.

3

Resilient System Design

Designed all systems for high availability with redundant power (including supervising solar installation) and network connections to overcome local infrastructure challenges.

4

Remote-First Operations

Implemented systems fully manageable from the US using Tailscale mesh networking and TeamViewer, providing real-time support across continents.

The Solution: Implemented Infrastructure & Systems

Core Infrastructure

Deployed HP EliteDesk Mini PCs as on-site Active Directory controllers and application servers, all managed remotely with redundant failover capabilities.

Enterprise Resource Planning

Led migration from expensive Sage software to self-hosted ERPNext instance running on Docker, currently in final testing phase with projected 70% cost reduction.

Custom Sales Platform

Built standalone, secure 'ledger' platform for remote sales data entry. Intentionally architected separately from main ERP to minimize attack surface and protect core financial data.

IoT & Cold Chain Monitoring

Developed and deployed sensor node system monitoring temperatures in remote refrigerators, ensuring product integrity across the supply chain with real-time alerts.

Security & Surveillance

Architected and supervised installation of 58-camera, multi-site CCTV network using modern IP cameras, replacing inferior contractor-led BNC system.

Fleet & Maintenance

Established preventive maintenance system for vehicle fleet and consolidated repair vendors, improving reliability by 40% and reducing costs.

Connectivity

Deployed multi-WAN modems and Tailscale mesh network ensuring stable, secure remote access for administration and testing with automatic failover.

Power Systems

Supervised installation of solar power systems providing resilient, cost-effective energy for critical infrastructure with 8-hour battery backup.
